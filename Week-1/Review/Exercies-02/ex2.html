GeeksforGeeks

GeeksforGeeks is a leading platform that provides computer science resources and coding challenges for programmers and technology enthusiasts, along with interview and exam preparations for upcoming aspirants. With a strong emphasis on enhancing coding skills and knowledge, it has become a trusted destination for over 12 million plus registered users worldwide. The platform offers a vast collection of tutorials, practice problems, interview tutorials, articles, and courses, covering various domains of computer science.

Company History :-- GeeksforGeeks was founded in 2008 by Sandeep Jain. His vision was to create a reliable and detailed platform for computer science education and skill development. Over the years, it has grown significantly and become a well-established name in the programming community.

GeeksforGeeks Revenue Model:-- GeeksforGeeks operates on a freemium model: Free Content: A significant portion of tutorials, practice problems, and resources are freely available. Premium Content: Access to advanced courses, paid certification programs, and exclusive content. Advertisements: The website monetizes through display ads. Corporate Partnerships: Paid job listings, sponsored contests, and employer branding opportunities.

GeeksforGeeks Target Audience:-- Students and Beginners: College students and individuals starting their journey in programming and computer science. Working Professionals: Individuals looking to upskill or prepare for interviews at tech companies. Competitive Programmers: Coders aiming to improve their skills for coding competitions and hackathons. Educators and Institutions: Universities and colleges looking for content and training resources for their students.

1. Programming Tutorials and Guides:-- GeeksforGeeks provides comprehensive programming tutorials in a wide range of languages, such as: C Programming C++ Java Python JavaScript HTML CSS SQL Ruby Go (Golang)
